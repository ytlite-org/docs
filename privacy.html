Privacy Information

Data Collection

YTLite does not collect or store any personal information about its users. All requests are made directly to YouTube's servers.

Cookies

YTLite does not use any tracking cookies.

Third-Party Services

YTLite proxies requests through its server, which means YouTube may see the server's IP address rather than yours. However, we don't control what data YouTube collects.

Logging

Server logs may contain basic connection information (IP address, timestamp) for maintenance and security purposes, but these are not linked to user activity.
